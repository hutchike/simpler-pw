Welcome to simpler.pw

Hate forgetting passwords? Me too. That’s why I made simpler.pw - a simple way to remember passwords, in a safe and secure way that’s different.

How is it different? Simpler.pw only does one thing: it transforms a memorable word into a secure password you can use as a login. When you need the password, just visit simpler.pw/password, enter the memorable word, and voila! Your password appears!

Here’s an example. If I type “tweet”, then click “OK”, I see the corresponding password “Yb3NPTlsb”. Maybe “tweet” is the memorable word that I use when I log into twitter.com just for example.

But how can you be sure your passwords are different than mine? You use a “master password” when you log into simpler.pw so that your master password is used to generate all your paswords from memorable words.

This simpler.pw website does not store or monitor any usage. It’s just a simple way to transform memorable words into passwords.

I sincerely hope it’s useful for you.
